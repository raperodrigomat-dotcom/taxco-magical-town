A Magical Town in the state of Guerrero, famous for its colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and silver craftsmanship admired worldwide.

History

Taxco is one of the oldest towns in Mexico. It is well known for its colonial heritage and its importance as a silver mining center since the colonial era.

Silver Tradition

Recognized worldwide for its silver jewelry, Taxco preserves this artisan tradition that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Tourism

Main attractions include the Santa Prisca Church, the cable car, scenic viewpoints, museums, and cultural festivals.
